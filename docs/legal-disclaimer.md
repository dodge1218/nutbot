## 3. Accuracy of Information

### Nutrient Data

All nutrient values provided in NutBot are **estimates** based on:
- USDA FoodData Central
- Manufacturer data
- General nutrition databases

**Actual nutrient content may vary** based on:
- Growing conditions, soil quality, seasonality (for produce)
- Cooking methods and preparation
- Brand variations
- Portion size accuracy
- Bioavailability (how much your body actually absorbs)

### Daily Value Percentages

Daily values (DVs) are based on general population averages and FDA guidelines. **Your individual needs may differ significantly** based on:
- Age, sex, and life stage
- Body size and composition
- Activity level
- Genetic factors
- Medical conditions
- Medications
- Pregnancy or lactation status

### Gut Health Score

The "Gut Health Score" is a proprietary metric created by NutBot for educational purposes. It is **not a medical diagnostic tool** and should not be used to self-diagnose digestive conditions, microbiome imbalances, or gastrointestinal diseases.

## 4. Supplement Recommendations

### Not Prescriptions

Any supplement products suggested by NutBot are **not prescriptions** and are provided for informational purposes only. 

**Before taking any supplement:**
- Consult your healthcare provider
- Disclose all current medications (supplements can interact with drugs)
- Check for allergens and contraindications
- Follow dosage instructions on product labels
- Purchase from reputable sources

### Potential Risks

Supplements can:
- Interact with medications (e.g., vitamin K with blood thinners)
- Cause adverse effects in certain populations
- Exceed safe upper limits if over-consumed
- Be contraindicated in specific medical conditions

**More is not always better.** Excessive intake of certain nutrients can be harmful.
